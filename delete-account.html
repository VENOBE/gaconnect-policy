EN

5. Account deletion

  • GAConnect user accounts are managed by your organization through hosted identity services.
  • Account deletion requests are handled through the dedicated web request URL linked inside the app.
  • In-app reset tools remove only local on-device data.

6. Contact

For privacy questions, contact your organization administrator or GAConnect support channel.

5. Hesap silme

  • GAConnect kullanici hesaplari, kurulusunuz tarafindan barindirilan kimlik hizmetleri uzerinden yonetilir.
  • Hesap silme talepleri, uygulama icindeki ozel web talep URL'si uzerinden yurutulur.
  • Uygulama ici sifirlama araclari yalnizca cihaz uzerindeki yerel verileri kaldirir.

6. Iletisim

Gizlilik sorulari icin kurulus yoneticinizle veya GAConnect destek kanaliyla iletisime gecin.
