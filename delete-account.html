Important: Deletion is irreversible. After completion, you may permanently lose access to account history and app services.

Process

3 steps
1

Recommended in-app method

If you can sign in, use the in-app “Delete Account” flow. It is typically the fastest option.

Tip: When you are already signed in, ownership verification is usually quicker.

2

Send a web request

If you cannot access the app, use the form on the right. The button opens your email client and pre-fills a compliant deletion request.

3

What happens next

  • Your request is reviewed to verify account ownership.
  • Your account is deleted from active systems.
  • Some records may be retained only when legally required.
