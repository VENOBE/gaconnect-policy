How to request account deletion

  • GAConnect user accounts are managed by your organization through hosted identity services.
  • Account deletion requests are handled through this dedicated web request URL linked inside the app.
  • If you can sign in, use the in-app account deletion flow first.
  • In-app reset tools remove only local on-device data and do not delete hosted account records.

Support

For account deletion or privacy questions, contact your organization administrator or GAConnect support channel.
