GAConnect Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-02-27

GAConnect is used by authorized field technicians to connect to industrial devices and read operational telemetry.

1. Data we process

Data Safety alignment (Google Play):

  • Personal info -> Email address: collected, not shared, required for account sign-in and account management, not processed ephemerally.
  • Personal info -> Name (profile/display name): collected, not shared, required for account management and in-app profile context, not processed ephemerally.
  • Personal info -> User IDs (account/session identifiers): collected, not shared, required for sign-in/session continuity and authorization, not processed ephemerally.

Additional in-app technical data:

  • Wi-Fi SSID, gateway IP, and network metadata used to verify connection to the intended field network.
  • Device serial identifiers used to keep device-specific history and selections separated.
  • Modbus telemetry stored locally on the device as CSV/NDJSON for diagnostics and export.
  • Technical service logs and alert events shown to the technician.

2. Why we process this data

  • To authenticate users and authorize access to organization resources.
  • To process Email address, Name, and User IDs for required account functionality and account management.
  • To establish reliable and secure connectivity to the intended gateway.
  • To preserve device-specific operational continuity.
  • To support telemetry collection and diagnostics while the app is active.
  • To notify technicians about critical status and fault events.

3. Storage and security

  • Sensitive tokens and credentials are stored in encrypted secure storage.
  • Authentication and API traffic are transmitted to required service endpoints over network connections.
  • Local telemetry files remain on the device unless the user explicitly exports them.
  • Users can clear local app data using in-app reset tools.

4. Permissions

  • Location: required by Android for Wi-Fi SSID and gateway detection workflows.
  • Nearby Wi-Fi devices / Wi-Fi state: required to inspect available target networks.
  • Notifications: required for critical status and fault alerts.
  • Current Android release does not request FOREGROUND_SERVICE permission.

5. Account deletion

  • GAConnect user accounts are managed by your organization through hosted identity services.
  • Account deletion requests are handled through the dedicated web request URL linked inside the app.
  • In-app reset tools remove only local on-device data.

6. Contact

For privacy questions, contact your organization administrator or GAConnect support channel.

GAConnect Gizlilik Politikasi

Son guncelleme: 2026-02-27

GAConnect, yetkili saha teknisyenlerinin endustriyel cihazlara baglanmasi ve operasyonel telemetriyi okumasI icin kullanilir.

1. Isledigimiz veriler

Data Safety uyumu (Google Play):

  • Kisisel bilgiler -> Email address: toplanir, paylasilmaz, hesap girisi ve hesap yonetimi icin zorunludur, gecici (ephemeral) degildir.
  • Kisisel bilgiler -> Name (profil/gorunen ad): toplanir, paylasilmaz, hesap yonetimi ve uygulama ici profil baglami icin zorunludur, gecici (ephemeral) degildir.
  • Kisisel bilgiler -> User IDs (hesap/oturum tanimlayicilari): toplanir, paylasilmaz, oturum surekliligi ve yetkilendirme icin zorunludur, gecici (ephemeral) degildir.

Ek uygulama ici teknik veriler:

  • Hedef saha agina dogru baglanmayi dogrulamak icin kullanilan Wi-Fi SSID, ag gecidi IP'si ve ag metadatasi.
  • Cihaza ozel gecmis ve secimlerin ayristirilmasi icin kullanilan cihaz seri tanimlayicilari.
  • Tani ve disa aktarma icin cihazda yerel olarak CSV/NDJSON olarak tutulan Modbus telemetrisi.
  • Teknisyene gosterilen teknik servis loglari ve uyari olaylari.

2. Bu verileri neden isliyoruz

  • Kullanicilarin kimligini dogrulamak ve kurumsal kaynaklara erisimi yetkilendirmek.
  • Email address, Name ve User IDs verilerini zorunlu hesap islevleri ve hesap yonetimi icin islemek.
  • Hedef ag gecidine guvenilir ve guvenli baglanti kurmak.
  • Cihaza ozel operasyonel surekliligi korumak.
  • Uygulama aktifken telemetri toplama ve tanilama sureclerini desteklemek.
  • Teknisyenleri kritik durum ve ariza olaylari hakkinda bilgilendirmek.

3. Saklama ve guvenlik

  • Hassas token ve kimlik bilgileri sifreli guvenli depolamada tutulur.
  • Kimlik dogrulama ve API trafigi, gerekli servis uc noktalarina ag baglantilari uzerinden iletilir.
  • Yerel telemetri dosyalari kullanici acikca disa aktarmadikca cihazda kalir.
  • Kullanicilar uygulama ici sifirlama araclariyla yerel uygulama verilerini temizleyebilir.

4. Izinler

  • Konum: Android'de Wi-Fi SSID ve ag gecidi tespit is akislarI icin gereklidir.
  • Yakindaki Wi-Fi cihazlari / Wi-Fi durumu: mevcut hedef aglari incelemek icin gereklidir.
  • Bildirimler: kritik durum ve ariza uyarilari icin gereklidir.
  • Mevcut Android surumunde FOREGROUND_SERVICE izni istenmez.

5. Hesap silme

  • GAConnect kullanici hesaplari, kurulusunuz tarafindan barindirilan kimlik hizmetleri uzerinden yonetilir.
  • Hesap silme talepleri, uygulama icindeki ozel web talep URL'si uzerinden yurutulur.
  • Uygulama ici sifirlama araclari yalnizca cihaz uzerindeki yerel verileri kaldirir.

6. Iletisim

Gizlilik sorulari icin kurulus yoneticinizle veya GAConnect destek kanaliyla iletisime gecin.
